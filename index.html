School Cafeteria

i ate my food that i brought from home to eat on the school benches.

The school cafeteria is the perfect place to take a break from studies and talk with friends. There are benches that multiple students can sit on in 1 row. You can eat lunch from your lunchbox that you took from home or order lunch from the menu each day with different brunches. On Friday, you have pizza! When you are done with lunch, the janitors can just fold the benches and roll them. The cafetaria has a stage that people can see. The stage is for the announcements that the principal can make. The cafeteria was built when the school was constructed just like the gym.
